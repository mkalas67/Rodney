# LinkedIn Post: Edinburgh

Edinburgh is home to one of the world's leading veterinary schools, a population that invests seriously in emotional wellbeing, and almost no certified pet loss practitioners.

For a city of this sophistication and professional depth, that absence is striking.

When a family in Morningside or Stockbridge loses a dog that has been part of daily life for a decade, the grief is genuine and often profound. Yet there is no established route to professional support. The vet offers sympathy. The GP is not equipped for it. General bereavement services are not designed for it.

A certified pet loss practitioner fills that gap, and Edinburgh is exactly the kind of city where that practice can thrive.
