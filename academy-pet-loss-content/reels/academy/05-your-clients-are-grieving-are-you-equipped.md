# 05: Your Clients Are Grieving. Are You Equipped?

**Pillar:** The profession exists
**Suno track:** Track-B
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

Your clients are grieving.

The ones leaving your practice
after a final appointment.

The ones in your therapy room
who keep circling back to their pet.

The rescue worker. The vet nurse.
The person who just needs to be heard.

Caring isn't enough
if you don't have the tools.

Not a criticism.
A genuine question.

Are you equipped?
