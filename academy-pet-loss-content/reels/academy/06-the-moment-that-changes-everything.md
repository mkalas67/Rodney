# 06: The Moment That Changes Everything

**Pillar:** The profession exists
**Suno track:** Track-C
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

There is usually a moment.

A client in front of you.
A loss you couldn't soften.

You said the right words.
You meant them.

But something felt incomplete.

Not failure.
Recognition.

The recognition that this person needed something
you hadn't been trained to give.

That moment is the beginning
of a different kind of practice.
