# 03: The Profession Most People Have Never Heard Of

**Pillar:** The profession exists
**Suno track:** Track-A
**Clip count:** 4

## On-screen text

There is a profession
dedicated entirely to pet bereavement support.

Trained practitioners.
A structured method.
Real outcomes.

It exists at the intersection
of grief counselling and human-animal bond research.

Most people have never heard of it.

That is changing.
