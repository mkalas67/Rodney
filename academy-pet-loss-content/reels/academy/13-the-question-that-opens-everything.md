# 13: The Question That Opens Everything

**Pillar:** The TRACE method
**Suno track:** Track-C
**Clip count:** 4

## On-screen text

There is one question
that changes the quality of a session.

Not a diagnostic question.
Not a clinical one.

"Tell me about them."

Not: how are you coping.
Not: how long ago did it happen.

Tell me about them.

It invites the animal back into the room.
It says: they were real. They mattered.

Simple.
Specific.
Generative.
