# 11: What Good Pet Bereavement Support Actually Looks Like

**Pillar:** The TRACE method
**Suno track:** Track-A
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

No platitudes.
No rushing past the loss.

A room where the animal's name is spoken.
Where the bond is treated as real.

Where someone asks how long you had them.
And actually listens to the answer.

Where grief is given a shape —
not minimised, not fixed.

That is what good support looks like.

It is learnable.
It is practicable.
It changes things.
