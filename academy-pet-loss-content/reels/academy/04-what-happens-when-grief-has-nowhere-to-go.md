# 04: What Happens When Grief Has Nowhere to Go

**Pillar:** The profession exists
**Suno track:** Track-C
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

Disenfranchised grief:
loss that isn't publicly recognised or mourned.

"It was just a pet."

Those three words
do more harm than most people realise.

Grief without a container
doesn't disappear.

It goes somewhere else.

Isolation. Shame. Prolonged suffering.

Pet loss is one of the most common forms
of disenfranchised grief.

We can do better than "just."
