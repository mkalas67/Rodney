# 10: The Difference Between Sympathy and Support

**Pillar:** The TRACE method
**Suno track:** Track-B
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

Sympathy says: I'm sorry for your loss.

Support asks: what do you need right now?

Both are genuine.
Only one creates movement.

Sympathy closes the moment.
Support holds it open.

The people working with bereaved pet owners
need more than sympathy.

They need a structure
that turns care into something useful.

That is the difference.
