# 20: What Our Graduates Say

**Pillar:** Who this is for
**Suno track:** Track-A
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

They say they feel prepared
in a way they weren't before.

That they no longer dread that room.
That they know what to do with the silence.

That clients leave sessions
having been genuinely held.

That they can tell the difference.

Not just more confident.
More useful.

That is what TRACE training produces.
