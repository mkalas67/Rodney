# 15: For the Therapist Whose Clients Keep Mentioning Their Pets

**Pillar:** Who this is for
**Suno track:** Track-B
**Clip count:** 4

## On-screen text

It comes up in session.
Not the presenting issue.
Not what they booked for.

But there.

A pet that died six months ago.
A pet that is dying now.
A grief that doesn't have a name in the model you're using.

You follow it, because that's what good therapists do.

But you wonder if you're equipped
to go where it leads.

You probably are.
A small amount of specific training
closes the gap completely.
