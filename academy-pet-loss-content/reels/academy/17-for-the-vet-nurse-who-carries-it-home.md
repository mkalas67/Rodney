# 17: For the Vet Nurse Who Carries It Home

**Pillar:** Who this is for
**Suno track:** Track-C
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

Vet nurses are in the room more than anyone.

Before the vet arrives.
After the family leaves.

They hold the animal.
They hold the client.

And then they prep the next room.

The emotional load is disproportionate.
The recognition, rarely so.

This work sees that.

You are not just support staff.
You are the person who was actually there.
