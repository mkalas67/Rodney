# 08: T Is for Trust

**Pillar:** The TRACE method
**Suno track:** Track-C
**Clip count:** 4

## On-screen text

The first thing a grieving person needs
is not information.

Not a process.
Not a timeline.

Trust.

The felt sense that this person is safe.
That they won't be judged.
That their loss is being taken seriously.

Trust isn't built in one moment.
But it can be lost in one.

T.
The first letter.
The first step.
