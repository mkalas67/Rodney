# 16: For the Person Who Lost Their Pet and Found Their Calling

**Pillar:** Who this is for
**Suno track:** Track-C
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

Some people find this field
because grief brought them here.

A loss that didn't have anywhere to land.
A search for something that could hold it.

And on the other side of that search —
a realisation.

This is what I want to do for others.

That journey often begins
at a place like Trace Memorial.
A space to honour what was lost.

And it continues here.
A way to turn that loss into something.
