# 18: For the Animal Rescue Worker

**Pillar:** Who this is for
**Suno track:** Track-B
**Clip count:** 4

## On-screen text

In rescue, death is part of the work.

Animals that don't make it.
Cases that come in too late.
Decisions that have to be made.

The grief doesn't stop accumulating
just because the work continues.

Rescue workers are rarely asked
how they're doing with it.

This training is not just for practitioners in rooms.
It is for anyone who encounters pet death
as part of what they do.

You are included here.
