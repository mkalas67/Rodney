# 09: Why Most Grief Support Gets This Wrong

**Pillar:** The TRACE method
**Suno track:** Track-B
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

Most grief frameworks weren't designed for pet loss.

They were adapted from human bereavement.
The fit is imperfect.

The social context is different.
The disenfranchisement is different.
The bond is different.

Applying a generic model
to a specific kind of loss
leaves gaps.

TRACE was built to close them.

Not a replacement for grief literacy.
A complement to it.
