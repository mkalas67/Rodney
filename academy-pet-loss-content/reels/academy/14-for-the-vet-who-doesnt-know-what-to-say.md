# 14: For the Vet Who Doesn't Know What to Say

**Pillar:** Who this is for
**Suno track:** Track-C
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

You've done this hundreds of times.

You know the clinical part.
You know how to make it peaceful.

And then the room goes quiet
and a person is standing there
and you don't know what to say.

You say something.
You mean it.

But you leave that room
still carrying something.

There are words.
There is a method.

You don't have to keep guessing.
