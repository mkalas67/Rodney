# 02: Why Vets Are Not Trained for This Moment

**Pillar:** The profession exists
**Suno track:** Track-B
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

The moment a pet dies
is one of the most emotionally charged in a vet's career.

Most vets are not trained for it.

Not because it doesn't matter.
Because it fell outside the curriculum.

Clinical skills: covered.
Grief support: mostly not.

A client leaves that room carrying something heavy.

The vet carries something too.

There is a better way to handle this moment.
For both of them.
