# 19: You Don't Need to Be a Therapist to Do This

**Pillar:** Who this is for
**Suno track:** Track-A
**Clip count:** 4

## On-screen text

A lot of people assume this work
requires a clinical background.

It doesn't.

Pet bereavement support is a distinct field.
The training is designed to be accessible.

Vets. Vet nurses. Rescue workers.
Counsellors. Social workers.
People who've experienced this loss themselves.

What matters is the commitment
to do it properly.

The door is open.
Wider than most people think.
