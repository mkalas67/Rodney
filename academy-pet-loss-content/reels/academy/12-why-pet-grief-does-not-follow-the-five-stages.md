# 12: Why Pet Grief Does Not Follow the Five Stages

**Pillar:** The TRACE method
**Suno track:** Track-B
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

The five stages of grief.

Widely known.
Widely misapplied.

Kubler-Ross developed them for people facing
their own death.

Not for bereavement.
Certainly not for pet loss.

Grief doesn't move in stages.
It moves in waves.

And pet grief moves in a context
of social minimisation that human grief doesn't.

A model designed for something else
applied to this
leaves people feeling more lost, not less.
