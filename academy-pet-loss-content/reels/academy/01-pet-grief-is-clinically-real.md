# 01: Pet Grief Is Clinically Real

**Pillar:** The profession exists
**Suno track:** Track-B
**Clip count:** 5

## On-screen text

Pet grief is clinically real.

Not metaphorically.
Not "almost like" losing a person.

Studies show the bond between human and pet
activates the same neural pathways as human attachment.

The same grief.
The same brain.

Disenfranchised grief:
when loss goes unrecognised by the world around you.

Pet loss sits squarely here.

The science says it belongs.
It always did.
