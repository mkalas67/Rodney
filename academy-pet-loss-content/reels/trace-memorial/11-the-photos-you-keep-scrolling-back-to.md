# 11: The Photos You Keep Scrolling Back To

**Pillar:** The memorial
**Suno track:** Track-E
**Clip count:** 3

## On-screen text

You know the ones.

The photo you scroll to
when you need to see them.

The look they had.
The light that day.

Those photos belong
somewhere safe.

Somewhere they do not get lost.
