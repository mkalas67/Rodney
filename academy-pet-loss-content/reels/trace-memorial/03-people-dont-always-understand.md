# 03: People Don't Always Understand

**Pillar:** The grief is real
**Suno track:** Track-D
**Clip count:** 3

## On-screen text

They meant well.

But "it was just a pet"
stays with you.

Some grief
is not given space.

That does not make it
smaller.

You do not need
permission to feel this.
