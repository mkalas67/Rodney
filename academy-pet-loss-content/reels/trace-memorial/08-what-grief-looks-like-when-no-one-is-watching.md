# 08: What Grief Looks Like When No One Is Watching

**Pillar:** The grief is real
**Suno track:** Track-D

## On-screen text

Early morning.

Before the day asks anything.

Just you,
and the missing.

This is where the real grief lives.

Not performed.
Not explained.

Just felt.
