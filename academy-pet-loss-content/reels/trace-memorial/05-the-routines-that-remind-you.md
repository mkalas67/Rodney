# 05: The Routines That Remind You

**Pillar:** The grief is real
**Suno track:** Track-D
**Clip count:** 4

## On-screen text

The walk you still
want to take.

The time of day
that was always theirs.

The food bowl
you haven't moved yet.

These are not habits.

They are the shape
of what you had.
