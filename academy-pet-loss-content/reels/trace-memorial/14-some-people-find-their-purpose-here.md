# 14: Some People Find Their Purpose Here

**Pillar:** Bridge
**Suno track:** Track-E
**Clip count:** 3

## On-screen text

Some people come through grief
and find they want to stay.

To sit with others
who are where they were.

To say:
I understand.
I am here.

There is a path
if you are looking for one.
