# Suno Track Prompts

Five shared tracks across both brands. Reference by track ID in each reel file.

## Academy for Pet Loss tracks

### Track-A
Warm ambient instrumental. Acoustic guitar fingerpicking, soft piano underneath. Gentle forward motion, unhurried. Earth tones in sound. No percussion. No vocals. 90 BPM. Warm and encouraging without being uplifting in a saccharine way.

**Suno prompt:** `warm acoustic fingerpicking, soft piano bed, gentle forward motion, ambient instrumental, no drums, no vocals, muted earth tone warmth, professional calm, 90bpm`

### Track-B
Slightly more presence than Track-A. Piano-led, subtle string texture. Thoughtful and considered. A sense of quiet intelligence. No percussion. No vocals. 80 BPM.

**Suno prompt:** `piano-led ambient, subtle string texture, thoughtful and considered, quiet intelligence, no percussion, no vocals, instrumental, calm and measured, 80bpm`

### Track-C
Quieter and more reflective than Track-A or B. Sparse piano, long reverb tail. Space between notes. A sense of looking inward. No percussion. No vocals. 70 BPM.

**Suno prompt:** `sparse piano, long reverb, introspective ambient, space between notes, quiet and still, no percussion, no vocals, reflective instrumental, 70bpm`

## Trace Memorial tracks

### Track-D
Very slow. Minimal. Single piano notes with long decay. Space is the main feature. A sense of sitting with something. No motion, no resolution. No percussion. No vocals. 55 BPM.

**Suno prompt:** `single piano notes, long decay, minimal ambient, slow and still, grief-tender, no percussion, no vocals, no resolution, intimate instrumental, 55bpm`

### Track-E
Slightly warmer than Track-D. Soft piano with very gentle ambient texture underneath — like a held breath. Still quiet. A small sense of warmth without being hopeful. No percussion. No vocals. 65 BPM.

**Suno prompt:** `soft piano, gentle ambient texture, warm and tender, held-breath quality, no percussion, no vocals, intimate instrumental, quiet warmth, 65bpm`
