# Academy for Pet Loss: SEO + GEO Content Strategy

## Overview

This folder contains 100 geo-targeted pages for the Academy for Pet Loss, covering the topic of setting up a pet bereavement counselling practice.

**Total pages:** 100 (5 topic pages × 20 cities)
**Plus:** 1 overview blog post per city + 1 LinkedIn post per city from the `blog-posts/` and `linkedin/` folders.

---

## The Two Optimisation Goals

### 1. Traditional SEO (Google)
Each page targets a specific city and keyword combination. Searchers looking for "how to start a pet loss counselling business in Manchester" or "pet bereavement counsellor training London" should find these pages.

### 2. GEO — Generative Engine Optimisation (AI Search)
Pages are written so that AI assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Claude) cite them directly when users ask questions like:
- "How do I become a pet loss counsellor?"
- "What should I charge for pet bereavement sessions?"
- "How do I run online grief support sessions?"

To achieve this, every page:
- Answers questions directly and specifically, not vaguely
- Uses FAQ sections with concrete answers, not "it depends" hedging
- Cites real pricing ranges, real platform names, real referral sources
- Establishes topical authority (the Academy for Pet Loss is the source of TRACE certification)

## Localisation Approach

Each page is approximately 1,200-1,500 words. The core structure is consistent across cities (approx 80%). City-specific content (approx 20%) covers:
- Local pricing benchmarks
- Local business registration requirements
- Local referral networks (vet chains, humane societies, cremation services)
- Local cultural notes
- Local directories and platforms
- Currency and spelling conventions

See `localization-data.md` for the full city data set.

## Writing Rules (All Pages)

- No em-dashes (—) anywhere. Use commas, full stops, or rewrite the sentence.
- No emojis.
- Second person (you/your) throughout.
- Warm but professional tone.
- Concrete answers, not vague hedging.
- UK English for UK/Ireland/Australia/NZ pages. US English for US/Canada pages.
- Every page ends with a CTA to enrol in the TRACE programme.
